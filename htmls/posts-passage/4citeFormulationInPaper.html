cite formulation

I am going to record how I cite formulation in paper. You know sometimes we forget how to do something and we are just search the internet. But how about we write the process ourself and just skim it when we need it and forget it. So this is the reson why I record the detailed process of how I cite the formulations in my paper.

First you should download the Mathtype and Mathpix snip tool. And may be Mathpix snip tool will be download along with the Mathtype. I don't remember though. Also you can use the free alternative to Mathpix. I think the cracked version of Mathpix would be better.

Fig.1. Mathpix Snipping Tool and MathType

Open your mathtype and mathpix snipping tool and then just go to the PDF file and find where the formulation lies.

Fig.2. find formulation in PDF and press shotcut

Now select the formulation area.

Fig.3. select the formulation area

The OCRED formulation will pumped in your screen.

Fig.4. pump the OCRed formulation

You should copy the OCRED formulation now. Just click the copy button lay in the first line.

Fig.5. click copy button in first line

And the go the you word file where you want to insert the formulation and click the MathType in the menu bar.

Fig.6. go to mathtype column

Now click your right number in the above list and then the formulation will be inserted with the index in the right side of the page.

Fig.7. click right number

The MathType interface will pump up in your windows after you finish the process 7.

Fig.8. pump the Mathtype interface

You should paste the copied formulation to the interface now and just click close after you paste and checked the formualtion is correct.

Fig.9. paste formulation and close MathType

Next you should put your cursor to where you want to cite and click insert cite in the list of MathType column.

Fig.10. put cursor to where you want put formulation cite and then click insert cite

Then the prompt will pump up to your window after you finish process 10.

Fig.11. pump the prompt after you click insert cite

The final stop is to double click the formulation number in the right side of the formulation.

Fig.12. double click the formulation number
