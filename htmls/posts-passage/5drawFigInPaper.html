Vetor figure

You will learn how to draw vetor figure yourself for your paper using drawing tool Visio.

First you should open a new page in visio.

Fig.1. create a empty page in visio

Then select the desired Figure from the paper pdf and copy the Figure.

Fig.2. select the img that you want to draw

Paste the paper to the empty visio page.

Fig.3. paste the img in the visio and adjust its size

Put the pasted Fig in the middle of the page and then imitate the Figure. First circle and then the irregular form.

Fig.4. just imitate the img that you wat to draw and use pencil to draw irregular form

After finish the regular form you should zoom in to draw the specific area.

Fig.5. spot the desired area to draw you irregular form

Make sure the irregular form is closed to let you fill some othre color. Usually we use pencil to draw the irregular form.

Fig.6. make sure the form you draw is closed to let you fill other color

Now fill the closed area some color.

Fig.7. fill the desired color

After you finish your framework you should use cursor tool to dray the Fig out of your drawing.

Fig.8. use curson tool to dray the img out of your drawing after finished the baseline

Now to click the little triangle of the form format to set the line style as no true line.

Fig.9. click the little triangle and set the line style no line to fit the desired design

Finally finish the detail and fill the midd circle and put it the top layer.

Fig.10. finally finish the detail and fill the middle cicle and put it the top layer

Part II:How to fill the irregular formation

After you finished you design which means you get the desired formation you should combine the form and the connect it: dev-tool--opration--connect. You use dev-tool to connect in order to make the form be closed.

Fig.11. combine the form after get desired form

Select the desired area and then go to dev-tool to connect.

Fig.12. select the desired area and then connect

But sometimes you may find there were some lines in the middle of the formation which you want to remove, then you can remove it using click the start--pencil. The form will show all the point in the form after you click pencil and click it.

Fig.13. remove certain part after combined just click pencil

Now use del keyboard to delete the point that you do not want in the form. And then fill you close form some color to test whether it is closed, if it is not closed, you should reclick the pencil and then then the form to adjust the point to make the form close.

Fig.14. now you can remove any point in the form

Part III: How to combine multi images be a beautiful one

When you want to use multi images to combined be a new image in visio. There are some tips to make your result beautiful. You should in the view tool open guideline and rule.

Fig.15. drag three line from left side to divide the screen to 3 part

Drag the rule from the bottom or from the top of the screen. To slice your screen to several parts. By this way, you will make the images connect with each other will no gap.

Fig.16. drag the image to the blank page and adjust

Put all your images that you want to combine in a folder first. If you have two screen, one screen show the files and another show the drawing window would be good. Just simply drag you images to the drawing and briefly organize them in the drawing.

Fig.17. first choose the desired experiment result to a folder and then drag them row by row

Now detailed organize them and after the first row is finished, add a horizontal line below the several images.

Fig.18. drag a horizontal line from top after lined a row

The arrange must be beautiful after you finished the design by above process.

Fig.18. the arrange is really good by setting horizontal and vertical line and adjust the image
